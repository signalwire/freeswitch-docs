# Test Numbers 

 

## About

If you want to test your PSTN carrier, or NAT settings, or network bandwidth or just general connectivity, there are several PSTN or SIP URIs available for your testing purposes.

Some other fun numbers on [voip-info](http://www.voip-info.org/wiki/view/Phone+Numbers). 

## USA

* 1-909-390-0003 - Ontario, California - Echo Test - No Ring, Immediate Echo

## Fax

## UK

## SIP URI Test

* `sip:904@mouselike.org` - Mouselike Sound Test - echo and others.
* `sip:echo@iptel.org` or `sip:3246@iptel.org` for Iptel's echo test
* `sip:hangup$HANGUP_CAUSE@conference.freeswitch.org` - trigger a hangup of your choice, *e.g.* `sip:hangupUSER_BUSY@conference.freeswitch.org`
