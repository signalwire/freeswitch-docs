## fifo_hold_music

string The music played to the consumer when they place a caller on hold by pressing `0`. It falls back to [fifo\_music](../FreeSWITCH-Explained/Dialplan/Variables-Master-List_16352570.mdx#fifo_music) and then the default hold music if `fifo_music` is not set.
